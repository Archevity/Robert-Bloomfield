Letter 306 - The Letters of Robert Bloomfield

306. Robert Bloomfield, Accounts * 

Receiv'd Jany 12. 1813 of Messrs Harris and Chater Assignees to the Estate of Charles Sharpe Eighty three pounds sixteen shillings and Nine pence, being a Devidend of Four Shillings and Six pence in the pound on my dept of Three Hundred and seventy two pounds, Twelve shillings, and Four pence.

Robert Bloomfield
