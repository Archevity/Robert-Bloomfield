Letter 53 - The Letters of Robert Bloomfield

53. Robert Bloomfield to Thomas Hood, 15 May [? 1801]* 

To Mr Hood

City Road. May 15th

Sir

The enclosed Letter contains some particulars which you ought to know; and on which, as you see fit you will confer with Mr Hill, to whom I suppose the M.S. will be sent.

Sir yours truly

Rob Bloomfield

Address: Mr Hood / Bookseller / No 31 Poultry — / Tuesday 3 Oclock
