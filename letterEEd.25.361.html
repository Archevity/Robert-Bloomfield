Letter 361 - The Letters of Robert Bloomfield

361. Robert Bloomfield to Mrs Bowden, 9 July 1822 * 

Shefford Beds July 9th 1822

Madam

I thankfully acknowledge the receipt of a Bank post Bill for £20 procured from the Literary Fund by the friendship & interest of Mr. Fitzgerald—I acknowledge it with the feelings which I hope may continue some time longer but I am sadly unwell and have only left for the good my blessing and my prayers.

Madam

Most respectfully

Your humble servant

Robt Bloomfield

Address: Mrs Bowden, / 17 Grosvenor Place, / London.

Notes

* BL Loan 96 (Royal Literary Fund) Microfilm 1077/10 RLF File 382. Copy; the original was returned to Mr Fitzgerald after being read at the General Committee of the Royal Literary Fund August 1822 BACK
