Letter 358 - The Letters of Robert Bloomfield

358. Robert Bloomfield to the Revd Edmond Williamson, 29 April 1822* 

Shefford, April 29. 1822.

Sir,

According to your Order I have sent you half a Dozen of my new work*  which reached me by coach a few hours past.

I have no remarks to make, and if I had, I am not in health and spirits to make them, but Am Sir, very truly, your Humble Servant,

Robert Bloomfield

Address: To the Revd Edmond Williamson / Campton
