Letter 330 - The Letters of Robert Bloomfield

330. Robert Bloomfield to Messrs Hodsall and Stirling, 17 November 1818* 

Gentn.

By this mornings post I received the sum of £40 in Bank post Bills, which sum you were requested by Walter Pye Esqr. to transmit to me as the gift of the Gentlemen Subscribers to the Literary Fund, And am

Gentn.

Your Obed Servt

Robt Bloomfield

Shefford, Beds.

Nov 17. 1818

Address: Sir Walter Stirling, M.P. / Strand / London

Notes

* Historical Society of Pennsylvania, Ferdinand Julius Dreer Collection, Collection 175, British Poets, The Historical Society of Pennsylvania BACK
