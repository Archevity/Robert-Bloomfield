415. Hannah Bloomfield to Robert Baldwin, 9 July 1828 *Â 

41 North Bank Regents Park

July 9th 1828

Sir,

My Mother desires me to request you will make up and send our account as soon as convenient if it can be in a day or two or within the present week it will the more oblige

Sir, yours &c &c

Hannah Bloomfield

Address: Robt. Baldwin Esq. / Baldwin & Cradock. / Paternoster Row. / July 9th.
