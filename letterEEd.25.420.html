London Dec. 2 1829

Madam,

We are very sorry to find that the sale of the works is now become so slack as to reduce the balance very slowly. Inclosed is a statement of the account from July 1 1828 to July 1 1829.

We are Madam, your obed servs

Baldwin & Cradock
